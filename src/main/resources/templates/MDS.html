Deck similarity

This section gives you information on similarity of decks being played with a certain identity. The information is also filtered for tournaments with deck pool up to a particular data pack / expansion.

Similar decks are placed close to each other on the chart, decks with a lot of differencies are spread out far apart.

Click a point on the chart and select See decklisk to get the list of cards of the deck.
