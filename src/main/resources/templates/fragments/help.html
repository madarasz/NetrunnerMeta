Cardpool

This section gives you information on which cardpools we have information on.

  • Tournaments: the number of tournaments we have information on.
  • Decks: the number of decks we have information on.
  • Rankings: the number of tournaments rankings we have information on. This consists of the identity information about a certain rank (i.e third place played Andromeda at the tournament).

We gather information from Acoo and Stimhack in an automated way.

Deck similarity / Average card usage

The chart gives you information on similarity of decks being played with a certain identity. The information is also filtered for tournaments with deck pool up to a particular data pack / expansion.

Similar decks are placed close to each other on the chart, decks with a lot of differencies are spread out far apart.

Click a point on the chart and select See decklisk to get the list of cards of the deck.
