Cardpool

Charts show the popularity of factions among the top decks over time. Time is represented by tournament legal cardpool.

Top decks are considered to be the decks played by the top 30% ranking players on each tournament.

Click on data pack / expansion name in available drilldown for more information.

Deck similarity / Average card usage

The chart gives you information on similarity of decks being played with a certain identity. The information is also filtered for tournaments with cardpool up to a particular data pack / expansion.

Similar decks are placed close to each other on the chart. Decks with a lot of differencies are spread out far apart.

Click a point on the chart and select See decklist to get the list of cards of the deck.

Average card usage gives you information on which cards are being played with this identity.

  • in decks: what percentage of all decks are using the card
  • average used: average number of the card being used counting on all decks
  • average used if in deck: average number of the cards being used, counting only decks having at least one of the card in question.
